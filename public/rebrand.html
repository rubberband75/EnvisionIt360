About Perry Homes

It all starts with you, our customer. From the very beginning over 40 years ago, Perry Homes has built affordable, thoughtfully designed, value packed homes for our customers throughout Utah. Always looking forward, we continue to develop communities where Utah families want to live. As a result of our ongoing pursuit of excellence in every phase of the home building process, many of our first time customers become second and third time customers.

Through prudent management practices, Perry Homes is a debt free and leverage free company. This fact allows us to pass savings on to you in the form of top tier quality construction at prices generally lower than our bank reliant competitors.
